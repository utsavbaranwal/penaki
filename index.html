About Us

At Penaki Luxury, our passion for the automobile is the engine that drives us. Born from a love for automotive artistry and engineering, we are a luxury lifestyle brand created by petrolheads, for petrolheads. We see cars not just as machines, but as rolling sculptures and pinnacles of human ingenuity. We believe that the beauty of a car extends beyond the road, and our mission is to capture that essence. We celebrate iconic designs through meticulously crafted sculptures and bespoke accessories. Our design process is a detailed journey, ensuring every curve pays homage to the original masterpiece. Each creation is a testament to our unwavering dedication to detail, quality, and the timeless art of the automobile, offering a tangible piece of automotive passion.
